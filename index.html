Heads up

PvP, raiding, and griefing are allowed.

There are no land claims and no anti-grief, so hide your base, protect your loot, and do not trust random players with shiny swords. No xray, hacks, lag machines, bug abuse, or spawn killing.

The vibe

Survival with actual risk.

gdevs server keeps the world player-driven. You get useful commands like homes, warps, RTP, TPA, kits, auctions, and money, but there is no overpowered /shop to make survival pointless.

Server features

Not just another empty SMP.

Useful systems, dangerous freedom, and enough chaos to make hiding your valuables a basic survival skill.

⚔

Bounty system

Place money on players, hunt targets, and make PvP more rewarding. If someone puts a bounty on you, maybe stop walking around like a loot piñata.

🍺

Brewery system

Use cauldrons, heat, brewing stands, barrels, aging, and experimentation to discover custom drinks. The best recipes are secret for a reason.

🎣

Advanced fishing

Fishing has custom catches, treasures, records, and tournaments. Sitting near water finally has a purpose besides pretending to be peaceful.

💰

Player economy

Pay players, check balances, sell through auctions, and trade without a giant server shop turning survival into an ATM simulator.
