Server rules

Simple rules. Real consequences.

gdevs server is semi-anarchy: PvP, raiding, and griefing are part of the world. The rules below only exist to keep the server fair, playable, and not reduced to a smoking console log.

Quick warning No claims. No anti-grief. Hide your base, protect your loot, and do not trust random players holding axes with suspicious confidence.
🛡
Fair play

Keep the server legit

  1. No hacking or cheating.Clients, macros, combat hacks, and unfair advantages are banned.
  2. No xray.No xray packs, xray mods, cave finders, ore tracers, glitches, or anything similar.
  3. No bug abuse or duplication.Report major bugs instead of turning the economy into a soup fountain.
⚔
World rules

Semi-anarchy rules

  1. PvP is allowed.Bring gear you can afford to lose. This is survival, not a museum tour.
  2. Griefing and raiding are allowed.No land claims means base security is your job.
  3. No spawn killing near protected spawn.Do not camp fresh players at spawn. Hunt people after they actually enter the world.
✦
Community

Do not be awful

  1. No hate speech or extreme harassment.Trash talk is fine. Being genuinely horrible is not.
  2. No lag machines or crash exploits.Do not build or abuse anything meant to damage server performance.
  3. Staff decisions are final.If staff steps in, listen. Arguing with gravity has better odds.
The short version

Raid smart. Fight fair. Do not cheat.

Everything that makes semi-anarchy fun is allowed. Everything that ruins the server itself is not.
