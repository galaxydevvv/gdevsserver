Server rules

Simple rules. Dangerous world.

This is semi-anarchy, not “do literally anything and ruin the server.” Tiny difference, massive improvement.

  1. No hacking or cheating.Clients, macros, combat hacks, and anything that gives unfair advantages are banned.
  2. No lag machines or crash exploits.Do not build machines or abuse mechanics to damage server performance.
  3. PvP is allowed.Fighting players is part of the server. Bring gear you can afford to lose.
  4. Griefing and raiding are allowed.No land claims means your base security is your responsibility.
  5. No xray.Resource packs, mods, glitches, or any xray-style advantage are not allowed.
  6. No spawn killing near protected spawn.Do not camp new players at spawn like a goblin with commitment issues.
  7. No hate speech or extreme harassment.Trash talk is one thing. Being awful is another. Do not be awful.
  8. Do not exploit bugs or duplicate items.Report major bugs instead of turning the economy into soup.
  9. Staff decisions are final.If staff steps in, listen. It saves everyone time and prevents console archaeology.
Allowed

Raiding, PvP, and griefing.

Hide your base, split your valuables, use homes carefully, and assume every stranger is either lost or preparing a betrayal arc.

Not allowed

Hacks, xray, dupes, and lag abuse.

Semi-anarchy still needs fair play. The chaos should come from players, not broken clients and exploit nonsense.
