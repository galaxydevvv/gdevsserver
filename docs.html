PvP guide

Bounty system

Bounties let players put money on another player’s head. If someone kills the target, they can claim the reward. It makes PvP riskier, louder, and somehow even more petty, which is exactly the point.

/bounty listSee all active bounties.
/bounty set <name> <price>Place a bounty on a player.
/bounty add <name> <price>Add more money to an existing bounty.
  • Do not carry your best gear if you have a bounty.
  • Bigger bounties attract stronger players.
  • Use homes, warps, and teleport requests carefully.
Plugin guide

Brewery guide

Brewery lets players create custom drinks through cauldrons, heat, brewing stands, barrels, aging, and experimenting. Some easy ideas are public. The stronger or stranger recipes are hidden so players have to test ingredients instead of reading a spreadsheet and calling it gameplay.

1. Set up a cauldron

Place a cauldron above a heat source, then fill it with water. Heat sources can depend on server config, but common ones include fire, lava, campfires, and magma blocks.

2. Add ingredients

Right-click ingredients into the cauldron. Cooking time matters. Too short, too long, or the wrong ingredient mix can lower the quality.

3. Bottle the brew

After cooking, use glass bottles on the cauldron to collect the unfinished drink.

4. Distill if needed

Some drinks need a brewing stand. Put glowstone dust in the top slot as the filter, then place the brew bottles in the bottom slots. Some recipes need multiple passes.

5. Age in barrels

Some drinks improve by aging. Try vanilla barrels or Brewery-style barrels. Wood type and aging time can change the result.

6. Experiment

Wheat, apples, potatoes, sugar cane, berries, and other ingredients can lead to different drinks. The rare recipes are secret. Good luck, tiny block chemist.

Starter drink hints

Try wheat-based drinks first. Apple-based drinks usually care about aging. Potato-based drinks may need distilling. Sugar cane can help with sweeter drinks. Exact advanced recipes are hidden on purpose.

Plugin guide

Advanced Fishing

Amazing Fishing makes fishing more interesting with custom fish, treasures, global records, personal records, and tournaments. Fishing can become a real money-making side activity instead of staring at water while pretending this is normal entertainment.

Custom fish

Catch more than vanilla fish. Some catches may be rarer, heavier, longer, or more valuable.

Records

Compete for global or personal records by catching impressive fish.

Tournaments

Fishing events can reward players based on catches, weight, length, or other tournament goals.

Treasures

Fishing can reward more than food. Watch for treasure catches and special loot.

Rod upgrades

Depending on server setup, custom rod enchants may improve your fishing experience.

Economy use

Fishing works with the server economy, but gdevs server still avoids an overpowered global /shop.

Money guide

Economy and auctions

The server economy is player-driven. Use money to trade, pay other players, buy through auctions, and place bounties. There is no global /shop because that would turn survival into “click button, win game,” and apparently we have standards.

/ahOpen the auction house.
/pay <player> <amount>Send money to someone.
/balCheck your current money.
/baltopSee the richest players.
