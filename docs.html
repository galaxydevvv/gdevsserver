Getting started

gdevs server is a semi-anarchy survival SMP. There are useful commands and an economy, but no claims and no anti-grief. Your base is only safe if you make it safe. Tragic, but educational.

PvP system

Bounties

Bounties let players put money on someone’s head. If another player kills the target, they can claim the reward. Simple, dramatic, and exactly the kind of problem Minecraft players invent for each other.

1. Check active bountiesUse /bounty list to see who is being hunted.
2. Set a bountyUse /bounty set <name> <price> to place money on a player.
3. Raise the rewardUse /bounty add <name> <price> to add money to an existing bounty.
Tip: If you have a bounty, do not carry your best gear like a walking donation chest.

Plugin guide

Brewery

Brewery lets players create drinks using cauldrons, heat, brewing stands, barrels, aging, and testing. A few beginner hints are public. The better recipes stay hidden so players actually experiment, a shocking concept in the age of instant guides.

Brewing process

Heat a cauldronPlace a cauldron above a heat source like fire, lava, a campfire, or magma if your server config allows it.
Add waterFill the cauldron with water before adding ingredients.
Add ingredientsRight-click ingredients into the cauldron. Ingredient choice and cook time affect the result.
Bottle itUse glass bottles on the cauldron after cooking to collect the unfinished brew.
Distill itSome drinks need a brewing stand. Put glowstone dust in the top slot as the filter and brew bottles in the bottom slots.
Age itPut drinks in barrels. Wood type and aging time can matter.

Barrels

Some drinks can age in normal barrels. Others may work better with Brewery-style barrels depending on server setup. Try different wood types and aging times if a drink comes out low quality.

Time conversions

Starter hints: Wheat-based drinks are good first tests. Apple drinks usually care about aging. Potato drinks may need distilling. Sugar cane can help with sweeter drinks. Advanced recipes are intentionally hidden.

Advanced Fishing

Amazing Fishing upgrades normal fishing with custom fish, treasures, records, tournaments, and more interesting rewards. Fishing is now a real side activity instead of sitting by water and pretending patience is gameplay.

Custom fishCatch more than vanilla fish, including rarer or more valuable catches.
RecordsCompete for personal and global records.
TournamentsEvents can reward players for catches, weight, length, or most catches.
TreasuresFishing can reward special loot and useful surprises.

Money guide

Economy and auctions

The economy is player-driven. Use money to trade, pay players, buy through auctions, and place bounties. There is no global /shop, because that would turn survival into a vending machine with trees.

/ahOpen the auction house.
/pay <player> <amount>Send money to another player.
/balCheck your money.
/baltopSee the richest players.

Survival guide

Staying alive

  • Do not build your main base close to spawn.
  • Hide valuables in separate stashes, not one giant griefable chest room.
  • Use /tpa carefully. A teleport request can be a trap wearing manners.
  • Check bounties before walking around in expensive gear.
  • Trade in safer areas and never pay first unless you trust the player.
